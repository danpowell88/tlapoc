Now built — the gap areas, prototyped

After a deep-research pass on each area below, we built POC flows for the parts a real AU clinic leans on every day — across Clinical, Operations, Finance, Team, Governance and Growth. The screens below are live; the deeper or riskier pieces stay sequenced as Roadmap/Research in the next section. (Client app excluded for now.)

Clinical · safety

Managing a complication

What happens: a filler client shows signs of vascular occlusion — minutes matter.

In the app: Clinical → Complication protocols launches the VO checklist, logs the hyaluronidase against the emergency kit and the client, and opens a routed adverse-event case + follow-up job. Anaphylaxis has its own protocol.

One model for every treatment

What happens: the clinic adds filler, laser, threads and a weight-loss program.

In the app: the treatment menu carries each modality's classification — S4 vs device, the DAEN route, the unit, rewards/ads rules — and blocks compounded GLP-1. One record drives charting, consent and adverse-event routing.

Reporting an adverse event

What happens: an NP logged a vascular occlusion after filler.

In the app: Governance → Adverse events classifies it as a device event (filler) vs the medicine path a toxin would take, prefills the report, lights the mandatory-trigger flag, and one tap opens the TGA portal and marks it submitted — all audit-logged.

Running a recall

In the app: Governance → Recalls already knows the exact clients who received that lot; the owner runs an SMS + call safety campaign and watches acknowledgements tick up. Policies sign-off, waste manifests, DSAR/breach drills and a one-click inspection pack are all here.

Growth · leads & reviews

Growth, without the compliance risk

What happens: enquiries pile up; a happy client posts a glowing "my Botox looks amazing" review.

In the app: a lead pipeline tracks enquiry → consult → converted; Reviews requests go to everyone (no sentiment gating) and replies are fine — but resharing the S4 review is blocked. Negative reviews are auto-flagged for follow-up (a job lands in the queue) and each can be acknowledged. The public booking page withholds S4 prices; email campaigns & social posting stay in your existing tools (Mailchimp, Meta). Settings → Integrations shows what's connected vs roadmap.

The full map — built & still to come

Being honest about the edges. v1 nails the compliant injectable loop; the gap-area pass above built POC flows for much of the rest (marked Built below). The remaining Roadmap/Gap/Research items are sequenced in the docs — sorted so nothing is a surprise.

Builtprototyped now (see "Now built") Roadmapplanned (Phase 2/3 — see docs) Gapreal need, not addressed yet Externalanother system handles it Researchfeasibility to validate

vaccinesTreatments & clinical

  • BuiltDermal filler & the modality model — multi-syringe/lots, filler-specific consent & device-DAEN routing (deep multi-area charting is Roadmap).
  • BuiltEnergy devices (laser/IPL/RF) — register, settings/fluence logbook concept & a state-laser-licence gate.
  • BuiltWeight-loss injectables (semaglutide/tirzepatide) — titration program & a hard compounded-GLP-1 block.
  • BuiltComplication management — VO / anaphylaxis response flow logging hyaluronidase & opening a routed AE case.
  • BuiltStandardised photography — fixed poses + ghost-overlay; outcome & revision tracking per treatment (3D imaging is Roadmap).
  • RoadmapOther modalities — threads/PDO, PRP, skin boosters, fat-dissolving, IV therapy (classified in the menu; deep charting later).
  • ExternalTelehealth video itself — recorded as consult metadata only.

support_agentFront desk & operations

  • BuiltWalk-ins & same-day add-ons — gate-respecting (an injectable walk-in is forced to a consult first).
  • BuiltWaitlist auto-fill — offer a freed slot to a scope-matched client in one tap.
  • BuiltDaily open/close checklist & twice-daily fridge log — excursions quarantine the lot & raise a breach job.
  • BuiltRooms / chairs / devices & utilisation; equipment maintenance register (autoclave/laser); inbound call log + missed-call text-back.
  • RoadmapHard resource conflict-blocking & deposits / card-on-file holds & no-show fees (opt-in, ACL-fair, cooling-off-suppressed — concept shown).

  • BuiltPricing & what-if + high-level reporting (revenue, mix, retention, MRR) — the commercial decisions stay in the app.
  • ExternalCommission / pay-splits & payroll — handled in Xero / a payroll vendor.
  • ExternalSupplier POs & accounts payable, refund reconciliation and BAS / GST — handled in Xero; invoices & payments sync from checkout.

  • BuiltRostering, shifts & leave — the roster drives who's bookable.
  • BuiltCredential + CPD + cosmetic-cover indemnity on a compliance board — a PII gap flags a nurse not-bookable for S4.
  • ResearchAHPRA register auto-verification (PIE) — approval-gated; a first-class manual-verify fallback ships either way.
  • RoadmapTimesheets & hours (rostered vs actual; no pay calc).

  • BuiltDAEN adverse-event routing — medicine vs device, prefilled + mandatory-trigger flag + portal hand-off (electronic submit is Roadmap).
  • BuiltRecall execution — lot→clients → SMS/call campaign → acknowledgement tracking.
  • BuiltPolicies & procedures sign-off; incident register; clinical/sharps waste manifests + IPC log.
  • BuiltAudit / inspection pack (one-click bundle) and DSAR (APP 12/13) + breach drill with 30-day clocks.
  • RoadmapElectronic DAEN/ASDER submission & state-EPA waste-system integration (prefill + recorded manifest today).

  • BuiltReviews / reputation — request-all (no gating), reply, acknowledge & auto-flag negatives for follow-up, S4-repost blocked; plus a lead/prospect CRM pipeline.
  • BuiltPublic booking page (generic service names, S4 price withheld) governed by the advertising linter.
  • ExternalEmail campaigns & social posting — handled in the clinic's existing tools (Mailchimp, Meta Business Suite), not rebuilt here.
  • RoadmapOnline checkout / deposits, two-way calendar sync, e-prescribing (ETP), deeper referral/affiliate, webhooks / public API.
  • ExternalMedicare / HICAPS / health-fund (mostly non-claimable for cosmetic); e-sign & payroll vendors.
  • ResearchIG/FB/WhatsApp messaging & injection auto-detect — feasibility to validate (24-h window, App Review, on-device ML).
  • GapClient app — two-way chat, NPS/satisfaction surveys, self-reschedule depth & waitlist opt-in.

Most "Roadmap" items are already sequenced in the requirements & phasing (Phase 2/3); the "Gap" items are genuine next-candidates worth a conversation; "Research" items need validation before we commit.
