Reception / front desk

Tess runs the front of house: she's the first and last person a client deals with — enquiries, bookings, the diary, attendance, checkout and chasing everything that's outstanding.

1 · An enquiry lands

"How much for lip filler?" on Instagram

What happens: a DM, a Facebook message, an SMS and an email all arrive in different places.

In the app: the unified Inbox shows every channel in one list, auto-categorised (Pricing / Booking / Complaint). She taps a suggested reply, tweaks it, and sends — or hits 🚩 Follow-up so it can't get lost.

In the app: Charting opens on a "with you now" banner + today's patient list. A patient whose consent isn't done is gated — she can't start until it's complete.

2 · Treating safely

Review → product → map → finalise

What happens: she sits the client down and works through the treatment.

In the app: the chart opens on a pre-treatment review (allergies, BDD, consent ✓, last treatment, "administering on a valid script") → she confirms the product & batch (only in-date stock) → maps the injection points → Finalise runs a close-out (aftercare, recall, 2-day wellbeing call) and deducts the units from that lot.
