What we don't cover (yet)

Being honest about the edges. v1 deliberately nails the compliant injectable loop; plenty of real clinic life sits outside that. Here's what a clinic does that the prototype doesn't fully handle — sorted so nothing is a surprise.

Roadmapplanned (Phase 2/3 — see docs) Gapreal need, not addressed yet Externalanother system handles it Researchfeasibility to validate

vaccinesTreatments & clinical

  • RoadmapDermal filler sessions — multiple syringes/areas & lots per visit, filler-specific consent & the device DAEN pathway.
  • GapEnergy devices (laser/IPL/RF) — scheduling, per-shot settings/fluence logbook, device safety & eyewear checks.
  • GapOther modalities — threads/PDO, PRP, skin boosters, fat-dissolving, IV therapy.
  • GapWeight-loss injectables (semaglutide/tirzepatide) — titration, supply & their distinct compliance.
  • GapComplication management — active workflow for vascular occlusion / anaphylaxis & hyaluronidase use (we hold the kit register, not the response flow).
  • GapStandardised photography — fixed angles/lighting, ghost-overlay, 3D imaging (we have basic before/after).
  • GapOutcome & revision tracking — touch-up/redo rates, satisfaction per treatment.
  • ExternalTelehealth video itself — recorded as consult metadata only.

support_agentFront desk & operations

  • GapWalk-ins & same-day add-ons — the flow assumes a pre-booked appointment.
  • RoadmapDeposits, card-on-file holds & no-show / late-cancel fees — deliberately out of v1.
  • RoadmapWaitlist auto-fill into cancellations (light today).
  • GapRoom / chair / device conflicts & utilisation (rooms are shown, not enforced).
  • GapEquipment maintenance — autoclave validation, laser servicing & calibration logs.
  • GapDaily open/close checklists & fridge temp logs (we alert on excursions, no daily log routine).
  • GapInbound phone calls — call logging / missed-call text-back (the inbox is digital channels).

paymentsMoney & retail

  • RoadmapPractitioner commission / pay-splits & payroll — important for contractor injectors.
  • GapSupplier purchase orders & accounts payable — we receive stock but don't order it.
  • RoadmapRetail (skincare) inventory & POS — barcodes, suppliers, stock counts.
  • GapRefunds, chargebacks & disputes handling.
  • RoadmapBNPL / payment plans (Afterpay etc.) & tips distribution.
  • RoadmapDeeper accounting / BAS — Xero export is planned (PRD-10).

badgeStaff & HR

  • GapRostering, shifts & leave — booking only hints at a roster.
  • GapAHPRA credential auto-verification & CPD / training records (we store credentials; the gate exists, not the CPD log).
  • GapTimesheets & hours.
  • GapIndemnity insurance tracking & expiry per practitioner.

verified_userCompliance & governance

  • RoadmapDAEN adverse-event submission — we prefill the export; no electronic submit yet.
  • GapIncident & mandatory-reporting case-management workflow (lightweight in v1).
  • GapPolicies & procedures document management & staff sign-off.
  • RoadmapFull infection-control + sharps / clinical-waste manifests & pickups (lightweight today).
  • GapRecall execution — we find lot→clients instantly; running the recall comms campaign is the next step.
  • GapAudit / inspection packs — one-click evidence bundle; data-access-request & breach drills (registers exist).

hubGrowth, integrations & the client app

  • GapReviews / reputation requests & monitoring; a lead/prospect CRM pipeline (enquiry → consult → conversion).
  • GapWebsite booking widget & SEO; social content scheduling.
  • RoadmapEmail campaign builder & deeper referral/affiliate (concept shown).
  • RoadmapOnline checkout / deposits, two-way calendar sync, e-prescribing (ETP), webhooks / public API.
  • ExternalMedicare / HICAPS / health-fund (mostly non-claimable for cosmetic); e-sign & payroll vendors.
  • ResearchIG/FB/WhatsApp messaging & injection auto-detect — feasibility to validate (24-h window, App Review, on-device ML).
  • GapClient app — two-way chat, NPS/satisfaction surveys, self-reschedule depth & waitlist opt-in.

Most "Roadmap" items are already sequenced in the requirements & phasing (Phase 2/3); the "Gap" items are genuine next-candidates worth a conversation; "Research" items need validation before we commit.
