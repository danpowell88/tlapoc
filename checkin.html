A tablet on the reception desk that checks clients in — by themselves

When a client arrives they tap the kiosk, find their booking, confirm their details, and complete anything still outstanding (medical history, today's health check, consent) before they sit down. Reception sees them appear on the arrivals board, and the treatment room knows they're here. It's the same self-service experience as the client app — just at the front desk. Tap through it; it's fully clickable.
