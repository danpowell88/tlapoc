The tablet the nurse uses in the room

A touch-first, safety-forward app for the clinician at the chair: pick up the checked-in client, run the pre-flight safety review, confirm consent and the prescriber's authorisation, chart the treatment on a tap-to-place injection map, capture before/after photos, and record exactly which vial and batch was used — then sign off and push aftercare to the client's phone. Try it — tap the face to chart.
