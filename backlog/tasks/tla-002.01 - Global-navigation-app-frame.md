## Description

<!-- SECTION:DESCRIPTION:BEGIN -->
**As a** staff member, **I want** a consistent left/top navigation to move between areas, **so that** I can get to the calendar, clients, clinical, stock and settings quickly.

The persistent navigation frame around every staff screen: primary areas, the active-area indicator, and the account/role menu. Navigation items are filtered by the signed-in role's capabilities (e.g. Reception does not see prescribing).

**Surface:** web (staff) · **Touches:** navigation, role capabilities.

**Build steps (functional):**
- [ ] Primary areas are reachable from a persistent nav
- [ ] The current area is clearly indicated
- [ ] Nav items are filtered by the signed-in role's capabilities
- [ ] Account/role menu (sign out, switch context) is present

**Prototype:** dashboard.png — left nav + top bar.
**References:** prototype PLATFORM area; capabilities model (ADR-0017).
<!-- SECTION:DESCRIPTION:END -->
