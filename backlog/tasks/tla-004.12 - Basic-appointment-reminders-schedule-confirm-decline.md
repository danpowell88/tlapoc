## Description

<!-- SECTION:DESCRIPTION:BEGIN -->
**As a** client, **I want** a reminder before my appointment that I can confirm or decline, **so that** I do not forget and the clinic knows if I am coming.

The booking-side scheduling of a single reminder (e.g. 24h prior) and handling its confirm/decline back into appointment status. This is the minimum reminder for MVP; the full comms engine (multi-step reminders, recall, campaigns, two-way inbox) is the later comms epic and will supersede the send mechanism.

**Surface:** web (staff) · API · **Touches:** reminder scheduling, confirm/decline -> status. Uses platform job scheduler; channel send stubbed/basic until comms epic.

**Build steps (functional):**
- [ ] Schedule a reminder relative to the appointment (e.g. 24h prior)
- [ ] Send the reminder via a basic channel
- [ ] Confirm/decline updates the appointment status
- [ ] Reminder activity is visible on the appointment

**References:** PRD-02 §6 (lifecycle reminders), REQ-BOOK-4, AC6; full engine -> PRD-07.
