## Description

<!-- SECTION:DESCRIPTION:BEGIN -->
**As a** front desk, **I want** to take payment in person by card or cash and issue a receipt, **so that** I can complete the sale at the desk.

In-person tender against an invoice: Square card-present and recorded cash, with partial/split payments, tips and configurable surcharge, producing a receipt. Both card and cash appear in the daily closeout.

**Surface:** web (staff) · API · **Touches:** payment capture, receipts, split/partial/tip/surcharge.

**Build steps (functional):**
- [ ] Take payment by Square card or record cash against an invoice
- [ ] Support partial and split payments
- [ ] Tips and configurable surcharge
- [ ] Issue a receipt; payment appears in the daily closeout
