## Description

<!-- SECTION:DESCRIPTION:BEGIN -->
**As an** owner, **I want** structured records of each practitioner's AHPRA registration, CPD and cosmetic insurance, **so that** the canInject gate is accurate and I am warned before anything lapses.

The staff Team records that feed the scope gate: AHPRA registration number/type/status/expiry/conditions, the 1-year-experience flag, CPD, cosmetic-cover PII, and engagement type (employee/contractor). AHPRA auto-verification (PIE) is an investigation item with a first-class manual-verify fallback. Alerts fire before expiry.

**Surface:** web (staff) · API · **Touches:** staff credential records, expiry alerting.

**Build steps (functional):**
- [ ] Capture AHPRA registration, type, status, expiry, conditions and experience flag
- [ ] Capture CPD and cosmetic-cover details and engagement type
- [ ] Records derive canInject and surface a clear current/at-risk/lapsed status
- [ ] Alert owners ahead of any expiry; manual-verify is always available
- [ ] (Investigation) AHPRA register auto-verification (PIE)

**References:** PRD-01 rev-4, REQ-TEN-6..9, C4, F13.
