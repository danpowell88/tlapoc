## Description

<!-- SECTION:DESCRIPTION:BEGIN -->
**As any** staff member, **I want** one profile showing a client's full history, **so that** I have everything I need in front of me before and during a visit.

The 360-degree client record: appointment history, contacts, medical flags and alerts, consents, before/after photos, memberships and balances. Tabs/sections compose data owned by other epics (clinical, payments). Money figures honour financial gating — Reception sees that a membership exists but not the dollars.

**Surface:** web (staff) · API · **Touches:** client aggregate view across epics, financial gating.

**Build steps (functional):**
- [ ] Overview: identity, contacts, key flags/alerts at a glance
- [ ] Clinical tabs: history, consents, photos (read views composed from clinical epics)
- [ ] Commercial tabs: memberships and balances, money figures gated to owner
- [ ] Quick actions: book, message, start visit

**Prototype:** client-360.png — overview + clinical/commercial tabs.
**References:** PRD-02 §4/§6 (360 profile), REQ-CLI-1.
