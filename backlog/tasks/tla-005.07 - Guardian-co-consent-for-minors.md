## Description

<!-- SECTION:DESCRIPTION:BEGIN -->
**As an** RN/NP, **I want** a parent/guardian to co-consent for an under-18, **so that** minors are treated only with proper authority and a recorded second consultation.

Guardian co-sign on the consent for under-18 clients, capturing the guardian's identity and relationship, alongside the mandatory second-consultation handling. Treatment for a minor is blocked until guardian co-consent is present.

**Surface:** client web/app · web (staff) · API · **Touches:** guardian consent, second-consult linkage.

**Build steps (functional):**
- [ ] Capture guardian identity, relationship and co-signature for under-18s
- [ ] Block treatment for a minor until guardian co-consent is present
- [ ] Link the recorded second consultation
- [ ] Surface minor + guardian status to staff

**References:** PRD-03 §4/§6 (under-18), C6; pairs with cooling-off.
<!-- SECTION:DESCRIPTION:END -->
