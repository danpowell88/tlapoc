## Description

<!-- SECTION:DESCRIPTION:BEGIN -->
**As an** admin, **I want** records to surface for destruction when their legal retention period ends, **so that** we keep records exactly as long as required and can prove disposal.

A retention engine and destruction register: retention periods (adults at least 7 years from last contact; minors to age 25; indefinite on complaint/litigation), records surfacing for destruction at end of life, and a destruction register with certificate references and a transfer log.

**Surface:** web (staff) · API · **Touches:** retention policy, destruction register.

**Build steps (functional):**
- [ ] Retention periods configured per record type, including minor and litigation holds
- [ ] Records past their period surface for destruction
- [ ] Destroying a record writes a register entry (patient, period, date, certificate ref)
- [ ] A transfer log records records sent elsewhere

**References:** PRD-01 §6/§8 (retention), C18, AC5.
<!-- SECTION:DESCRIPTION:END -->

## Acceptance Criteria
