## Description

<!-- SECTION:DESCRIPTION:BEGIN -->
**As an** owner, **I want** an omnichannel inbox, reviews/reputation and a lead CRM, **so that** the clinic grows from one place — within advertising rules.

Phase-2 growth surfaces (spec'd now): an omnichannel inbox (IG/FB/SMS/email) as reactive/service channels with client linking and templated/keyword suggested replies (no AI; no marketing DMs); reviews/reputation (request-all, reply-yes, with a caution against resharing S4-endorsing testimonials; negative/complaint reviews auto-raise jobs); and a lead/prospect CRM over the inbox. All share Spam-Act consent.

**Surface:** web (staff) · API · external (Meta/SMS/email) · **Touches:** inbox channels, reviews, leads.

**Build steps (functional):**
- [ ] Omnichannel reactive inbox with client linking + suggested replies (no AI)
- [ ] Reviews/reputation: request, reply, caution on S4 testimonials, auto-raise jobs on negatives
- [ ] Lead/prospect CRM over the inbox
- [ ] No proactive marketing DMs; proactive marketing stays SMS/email

**References:** PRD-07 rev-2/4 (growth), REQ-NOTIF-6/8/9, ADR-0018/0019/0032/0033.
