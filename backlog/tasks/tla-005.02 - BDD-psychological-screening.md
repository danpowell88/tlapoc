## Description

<!-- SECTION:DESCRIPTION:BEGIN -->
**As an** RN/NP, **I want** a body-dysmorphia / psychological screen completed and flagged before treatment, **so that** I can pause and reassess when someone may not benefit from a cosmetic procedure.

A screening instrument completed as part of intake, scored to raise a flag for prescriber review. A positive flag is surfaced prominently to the prescriber before treatment can proceed, and the completed screen is part of the record.

**Surface:** client web/app · web (staff) · API · **Touches:** screening instrument/result, prescriber flag.

**Build steps (functional):**
- [ ] Client completes the screening questionnaire as part of intake
- [ ] Responses are scored and a flag derived
- [ ] A positive flag is surfaced to the prescriber before treatment
- [ ] The completed screen is retained on the record

**Prototype:** intake BDD/wellbeing step.
**References:** PRD-03 §6 (BDD), REQ-CONS-2, C3, AC2; instrument choice is an open question.
