## Description

<!-- SECTION:DESCRIPTION:BEGIN -->
**As a** client, **I want** to read plain-language consent and e-sign it, **so that** I understand and agree to the procedure, its risks, alternatives, my practitioner's qualifications and the cost.

Built-in e-signed consent against a versioned template. The presented consent carries the mandated content (nature of procedure, risks/benefits/alternatives, practitioner qualifications, costs, realistic-outcome language, complaint mechanisms including the right to complain to AHPRA despite any NDA). The signed consent is bound to the exact template version signed.

**Surface:** client web/app · web (staff) · API · **Touches:** consent signature, template version binding.

**Build steps (functional):**
- [ ] Present the current consent template in plain language
- [ ] Client e-signs; capture is recorded with timestamp and identity
- [ ] The signature is bound to the template version signed
- [ ] Consent status is visible to staff on the visit

**Prototype:** forms-consent.png — consent reader + e-sign.
**References:** PRD-03 §6 (consent), REQ-CONS-1, C5, AC1/AC6.
