## Description

<!-- SECTION:DESCRIPTION:BEGIN -->
**As an** owner, **I want** SMS, email and push channels with per-tenant templates, **so that** the clinic can send consistent, branded messages.

The messaging backbone: a notifier over an AU SMS provider, email and app push, with per-tenant message templates. Every other comms feature sends through this.

**Surface:** API · web (staff) · **Touches:** notifier (SMS/email/push), templates, comms log.

**Build steps (functional):**
- [ ] Send over SMS, email and push behind one notifier interface
- [ ] Per-tenant editable templates
- [ ] Every send is logged to the client's comms history

**References:** PRD-07 §6, REQ-NOTIF-1, ADR-0012, AC5.
<!-- SECTION:DESCRIPTION:END -->
