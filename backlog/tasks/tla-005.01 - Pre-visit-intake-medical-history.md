## Description

<!-- SECTION:DESCRIPTION:BEGIN -->
**As a** client, **I want** to complete my medical history, medications, allergies and contraindications before my visit, **so that** my practitioner can treat me safely.

The digital pre-visit intake the client completes on their phone (link sent at booking): medical history, current medications, allergies and contraindications. A returning client gets a quick re-screen rather than the full form. Responses auto-link to the visit and the chart and surface contraindication flags to the practitioner.

**Surface:** client web/app · web (staff) · API · **Touches:** intake form/response, contraindication flags, chart link.

**Build steps (functional):**
- [ ] Client completes history, meds, allergies and contraindications before the visit
- [ ] Returning clients get a shorter re-screen
- [ ] Responses link to the appointment and the client's chart
- [ ] Contraindication/risk flags surface to staff
- [ ] Staff can see intake status (not sent / sent / completed) per visit

**Prototype:** forms-consent.png / intake wizard.
**References:** PRD-03 §6 (intake), REQ-CONS-2/3, C3.
