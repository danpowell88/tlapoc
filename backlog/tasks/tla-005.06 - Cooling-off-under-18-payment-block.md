## Description

<!-- SECTION:DESCRIPTION:BEGIN -->
**As the** clinic, **I want** the system to enforce a cooling-off period and payment block for under-18s, **so that** minors get a mandatory pause and a second consult before a cosmetic procedure.

For an under-18: enforce at least 7 days between consent and procedure, block payment (except the consult) until it elapses, and record the offer of a second consultation. Adult cooling-off is configurable (default off, pending legal position). Cooling-off also suppresses any booking deposit/hold.

**Surface:** web (staff) · API · **Touches:** cooling-off timer, payment block, second-consult record.

**Build steps (functional):**
- [ ] Under-18: enforce >=7 days between consent and procedure
- [ ] Block payment (except the consult) until cooling-off elapses
- [ ] Record a second-consultation offer
- [ ] Adult cooling-off configurable (default off)
- [ ] Surface cooling-off state to staff and suppress deposits during it

**Prototype:** cooling-off chip / payment block.
**References:** PRD-03 §6 (cooling-off), REQ-CONS-4, C6, AC3; coordinates with PRD-06.
<!-- SECTION:DESCRIPTION:END -->

## Acceptance Criteria
<!-- AC:BEGIN -->

- [ ] #2 A second-consultation offer is recorded for under-18s
