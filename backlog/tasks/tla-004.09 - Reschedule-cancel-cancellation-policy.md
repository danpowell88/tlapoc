## Description

<!-- SECTION:DESCRIPTION:BEGIN -->
**As a** client or front desk, **I want** to reschedule or cancel within a clear policy, **so that** changes are easy and the clinic's policy is applied consistently.

Self-service and desk reschedule/cancel governed by a configurable cancellation/no-show policy. In MVP there is no auto-charge on late cancellation (deposits/holds are a separate later story); the policy is displayed and recorded. Cancelling a slot offers it to the waitlist.

**Surface:** web (staff) · client · API · **Touches:** appointment reschedule/cancel, policy config.

**Build steps (functional):**
- [ ] Configure a cancellation/no-show policy (windows, rules)
- [ ] Client can self-reschedule/cancel within policy
- [ ] Desk can reschedule/cancel any appointment
- [ ] Policy is displayed at booking and applied on change (no auto-charge in MVP)
- [ ] A cancelled slot is offered to the waitlist

**References:** PRD-02 §6 (policy/lifecycle), REQ-BOOK-3/4, AC3.
