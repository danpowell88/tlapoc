## Description

<!-- SECTION:DESCRIPTION:BEGIN -->
**As an** owner, **I want** revenue, retention and operational dashboards filterable by date and practitioner, **so that** I can run the business on real numbers.

The owner business intelligence: revenue (service/product/membership), retention/churn, no-shows, cancellations, conversion funnel, at-risk and big spenders, membership MRR/churn, per-practitioner mix, with date-range filters. Money figures are owner-only (financial gating). Reads from read-models/materialised views, not the transactional store.

**Surface:** web (owner) · API · **Touches:** reporting read-models, date filters, financial gating.

**Build steps (functional):**
- [ ] Revenue, retention/churn, no-shows, cancellations, conversion, MRR dashboards
- [ ] Date-range presets + custom; per-practitioner views
- [ ] Money figures owner-only behind financial gating
- [ ] Read from materialised views for performance

**Prototype:** reports.png.
**References:** PRD-08 §6, REQ-RPT-1/2, AC6/AC7, ADR-0013.
