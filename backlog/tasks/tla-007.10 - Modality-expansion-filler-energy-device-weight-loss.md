## Description

<!-- SECTION:DESCRIPTION:BEGIN -->
**As an** injector/therapist, **I want** charting for filler, energy devices and weight-loss programs, **so that** the platform covers our full treatment menu beyond toxin.

Phase-2 charting modalities (spec'd now): dermal filler (multi-area / multi-syringe / per-area lot + a mandatory vascular-occlusion/blindness consent gate); energy-device charting (per-pass settings/fluence logbook + skin-type/patch-test + safety checks, gated by a state laser licence); weight-loss titration plans; and standardised photo capture with ghost-overlay alignment.

**Surface:** web (staff) · provider app · API · **Touches:** modality-aware chart entries, consent/licence gates.

**Build steps (functional):**
- [ ] Filler charting: multi-area/multi-syringe, per-area lot, mandatory VO/blindness consent gate
- [ ] Energy-device charting: per-pass settings/fluence log, patch-test/skin-type, laser-licence gate
- [ ] Weight-loss titration plans
- [ ] Ghost-overlay photo alignment

**References:** PRD-05 rev-4 (modality model), REQ-CLIN-10..13, ADR-0025.
