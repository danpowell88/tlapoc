## Description

<!-- SECTION:DESCRIPTION:BEGIN -->
**As a** client, **I want** my membership auto-charged on schedule from a stored card, **so that** I keep my benefits without manual payments.

Recurring autopay via a tokenised card-on-file (added online/in-app or at the desk), charging on schedule, with dunning/retry on a failed charge. Built on the payment-provider abstraction; proven by the Square AU recurring spike.

**Surface:** web (staff) · client · API · **Touches:** card-on-file token, recurring charge, dunning.

**Build steps (functional):**
- [ ] Store a card-on-file token (added online/in-app or at desk)
- [ ] Charge the membership automatically on schedule
- [ ] Dunning/retry on a failed charge, with client notification
- [ ] No card number stored, only tokens

**References:** PRD-06 §6, REQ-MEMB-1, AC2; Square AU recurring spike.
