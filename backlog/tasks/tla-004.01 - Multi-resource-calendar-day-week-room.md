## Description

<!-- SECTION:DESCRIPTION:BEGIN -->
**As a** front desk, **I want** a day/week/room calendar showing practitioners and rooms with correct durations and buffers, **so that** I can run the diary at a glance and book without clashes.

The operational heart of reception: a diary that books a practitioner AND a room/chair/device together, every service carrying its real duration plus buffer/processing/turnaround. Availability is never raw roster — it is the intersection of the practitioner's rostered shift (minus time-off and existing bookings), their canInject scope for the service, and a free room/chair/device for the whole block. This shared availability engine is reused by the wizard, online booking, walk-ins and waitlist.

**Surface:** web (staff) · API · **Touches:** appointments, resources (room/chair/device), availability engine (roster x canInject x resource).

**Build steps (functional):**
- [ ] Day, week and room views render practitioner and room columns
- [ ] Services apply real durations including buffer/processing/turnaround
- [ ] Availability = rostered shift, minus time-off/bookings, AND canInject for the service, AND a free resource
- [ ] Drag to move recomputes availability at the target and flags room/chair/device conflicts
- [ ] Per-day and per-treatment-type counts + chair utilisation are surfaced

**Prototype:** schedule.png — Day/Week toggle, colour-coded blocks, utilisation strip.
**References:** PRD-02 §6 (calendar), REQ-BOOK-1, C4, ADR-0024/0026/0029.
