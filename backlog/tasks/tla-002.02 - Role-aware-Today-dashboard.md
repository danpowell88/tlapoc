## Description

<!-- SECTION:DESCRIPTION:BEGIN -->
**As a** staff member, **I want** the app to open on a 'Today' view tailored to my role, **so that** I immediately see what needs my attention this shift.

The landing dashboard the clinic opens on, assembled from role-relevant cards: today's appointments and their status, things needing action (incomplete intake/consent, waiting clients, follow-ups), and quick actions. Content is driven by the role's concerns — Reception sees the desk, a nurse sees their list, the owner sees the clinic. Money figures only appear for owner per the financial-gating rule.

**Surface:** web (staff) · **Touches:** today aggregation, role concerns, financial gating.

**Build steps (functional):**
- [ ] A 'Today' landing view assembled from role-relevant cards
- [ ] Surfaces today's appointments and their statuses
- [ ] Surfaces action items (incomplete intake/consent, waiting, follow-ups due)
- [ ] Card set varies by role; money figures honour financial gating
- [ ] Quick actions (new booking, find client) are one click away

**Prototype:** dashboard.png — Today statcards, pills, digest.
**References:** prototype PLATFORM/Today; concerns model (ADR-0017).
<!-- SECTION:DESCRIPTION:END -->
