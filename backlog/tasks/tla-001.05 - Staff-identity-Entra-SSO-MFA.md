## Description

<!-- SECTION:DESCRIPTION:BEGIN -->
**As a** staff member, **I want** to sign in with our existing Microsoft 365 account and MFA, **so that** I get a secure, scoped session without a separate password.

Wires staff authentication to Entra ID single-sign-on with multi-factor. The session is scoped to the staff member's tenant; role and scope enforcement are layered on in the identity epic. An owner can invite staff who then sign in with their M365 accounts.

**Surface:** web (staff) · API · **Touches:** Entra ID, session/tenant context.

**Build steps (functional):**
- [ ] Staff sign in via Microsoft 365 / Entra single sign-on
- [ ] Multi-factor is enforced on sign-in
- [ ] The resulting session is scoped to the staff member's tenant
- [ ] An owner can invite a staff member who can then sign in

**References:** PRD-01 §4/§6 (identity), AC3.
