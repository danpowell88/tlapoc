## Description

<!-- SECTION:DESCRIPTION:BEGIN -->
**As a** prescriber/owner, **I want** to raise purchase orders and receive stock against them with ARTG verification, **so that** S4 medicine only ever enters from a lawful source.

Supplier purchase orders and receiving: an S4 PO requires a prescriber signer and a TGA-approved wholesaler; receiving confirms ARTG status, brand, sponsor and lawful supply source, and warns or blocks non-ARTG/unverified-source stock per configuration. Retail (non-S4) receiving is lighter.

**Surface:** web (staff) · API · **Touches:** purchase orders, receiving, ARTG/supply-source verification.

**Build steps (functional):**
- [ ] Raise a purchase order; S4 POs require a prescriber signer + TGA-approved wholesaler
- [ ] Receive stock against a PO into the ledger with lot/expiry
- [ ] Confirm ARTG status, brand, sponsor and supply source on receipt
- [ ] Warn/block non-ARTG or unverified-source stock per config

**References:** PRD-04 rev-4 + §6, REQ-MED-6/12, C11, AC7.
