## Description

<!-- SECTION:DESCRIPTION:BEGIN -->
**As an** owner, **I want** to build a custom role from capabilities, **so that** I can match my clinic's specific job structure beyond the standard presets.

A future addition to RBAC: compose a role from the capability set and assign it to staff, beyond the built-in presets (Solo, Nurse-led, etc.).

**Surface:** web (staff) · API · **Touches:** role composition.

**Build steps (functional):**
- [ ] Compose a custom role by selecting capabilities
- [ ] Assign the custom role to staff
- [ ] Custom roles behave identically to built-in roles at enforcement time

**References:** PRD-01 (REQ-TEN-5, future), ADR-0017.
