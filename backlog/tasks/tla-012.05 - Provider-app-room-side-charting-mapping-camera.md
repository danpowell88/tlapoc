## Description

<!-- SECTION:DESCRIPTION:BEGIN -->
**As an** injector, **I want** to map injections and capture photos room-side, **so that** I chart at the chair without a desktop.

Surfaces charting/mapping and native camera capture on the provider app, with photos to central storage via signed links and never persisted on the device beyond a transient cache; capture requires image-use consent. Offline tolerance is delivered by the charting offline-sync story.

**Surface:** Flutter (provider) · API · **Touches:** mapping canvas, camera capture, signed-URL media.

**Build steps (functional):**
- [ ] Map injection points on diagram/photo room-side
- [ ] Native camera capture to central storage via signed links
- [ ] Require image-use consent; no on-device persistence beyond sync cache
- [ ] Finalise read-only once server-side finalisation completes

**References:** PRD-09 §6, REQ-APP-2/3, C14, AC2/AC6, ADR-0009; pairs with TLA-007 offline sync.
