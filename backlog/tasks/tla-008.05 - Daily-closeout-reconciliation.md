## Description

<!-- SECTION:DESCRIPTION:BEGIN -->
**As a** front desk/owner, **I want** an end-of-day closeout that balances card and cash, **so that** the drawer reconciles and the day's takings are confirmed.

The daily closeout: totals card and cash tenders for the day, supports counting the drawer, and flags discrepancies. Operational for the desk (counting takings); clinic-wide revenue reporting remains the owner-only reporting epic.

**Surface:** web (staff) · API · **Touches:** closeout totals, reconciliation.

**Build steps (functional):**
- [ ] Total card and cash tenders for the day
- [ ] Count the drawer and reconcile against expected
- [ ] Flag and note discrepancies
- [ ] Close the day; closed days are locked
