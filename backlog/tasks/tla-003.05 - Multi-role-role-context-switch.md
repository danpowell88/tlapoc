## Description

<!-- SECTION:DESCRIPTION:BEGIN -->
**As a** staff member who holds more than one role (e.g. RN who also covers reception), **I want** to switch my active role, **so that** my screens and permissions match what I am doing right now.

Support for a person holding multiple roles, with a clear active-role indicator and a switch. The active role drives capabilities and the Today dashboard concerns.

**Surface:** web (staff) · **Touches:** role assignment, active-role context.

- [ ] A staff member can be assigned more than one role
