## Description

<!-- SECTION:DESCRIPTION:BEGIN -->
**As a** client, **I want** to only receive marketing I opted into and always be able to unsubscribe, **so that** my consent is respected.

Spam-Act compliance: opt-in for commercial electronic messages, sender identification, a functional unsubscribe, and immediate suppression on withdrawal. Transactional messages (reminders/aftercare) are exempt and always send.

**Surface:** API · client · web (staff) · **Touches:** marketing consent, suppression list, unsubscribe.

**Build steps (functional):**
- [ ] Capture marketing opt-in with sender identification
- [ ] Marketing sends only to opted-in clients with a working unsubscribe
- [ ] Unsubscribe suppresses future marketing immediately
- [ ] Transactional messages remain exempt

**References:** PRD-07 §6, REQ-NOTIF-5, C23, AC1.
<!-- SECTION:DESCRIPTION:END -->

## Acceptance Criteria
<!-- AC:BEGIN -->
- [ ] #1 A marketing message sends only to opted-in clients and always includes a working unsubscribe
- [ ] #2 Unsubscribing suppresses future marketing immediately; transactional messages are unaffected
