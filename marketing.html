Questions

Good to know.

Is this a real, live product?add

This is a concept with made-up sample data. It shows how friendly, all-in-one software for an aesthetic clinic would look and feel from end to end.

Is it hard to learn?add

Not at all — it's built for clinic teams, not IT. The screens follow how your day actually flows, so most people are comfortable within a morning.

Does it keep us on the right side of the rules?add

Yes — the safety paperwork is built in and designed around the Australian rules for cosmetic clinics (TGA, AHPRA, S4 medicines and the Privacy Act), so the right boxes get ticked as you go.

Does it work on mobile and tablet?add

Yes — it's lovely on a tablet in the treatment room, with apps for your team and your clients for booking, consent and notes on the go.

Can I see how it actually works?add

Absolutely. Open the interactive demo, take the day-in-the-life tour, or browse the full documentation.
