Compliant by configuration

  • •Prices are withheld; S4 (prescription) services are always price-free and gated to "consultation required".
  • •Only generic, clinic-set public names — no S4 brands or "anti-wrinkle injections".
  • •Scope-aware: prescription treatments only offer RN / NP practitioners.
  • •No deposit or card-on-file (v1) — it captures the request, the clinic confirms.
integration_instructions

How embedding works

  • •The widget always renders in a sandboxed iframe — the clinic's CSS can't break it, and it can't touch theirs.
  • •The frame auto-resizes to its content via postMessage — no scrollbars.
  • •Simple theming (colour, radius, names) rides in the URL; richer config is pushed via data-config.
  • •A tla:booking event lets the host site react (analytics, redirect, etc.).

All names, services and bookings are synthetic — no real client, staff or business data. Production embed URLs point at danpowell88.github.io/tlapoc/.
